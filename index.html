Bio

Hi, I'm Charlie. I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow at Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health and Dana Farber Cancer Institute in Boston, MA. I'm originally from Manchester, UK. I use geospatial tools to link physical, chemical, and social environmental exposures to large health datasets (e.g. individual-level cohort studies and multi-state cancer registries). I also do stats. My career goal is environmetal equity.
